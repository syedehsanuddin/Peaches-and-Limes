Our Story

Peaches and Limes began as a shared passion between two individuals, Ahsan and Tayyebah. Their love for mugs brought them together, leading them to explore the world of ceramics. What started as a simple interest quickly turned into a deep dive, as they enrolled in ceramic classes to perfect their craft.

Ahsan and Tayyebah, combined their unique skill sets and creative minds to establish Peaches and Limes. Their vision was clear from the start: to create exclusive ceramic pieces that stand out in quality and design.

"Our journey with ceramics has been as much about the art as it has been about the joy of creating something together." - Ahsan & Tayyebah

Today, Peaches and Limes offers a collection of carefully crafted ceramic pieces that reflect their dedication to the craft and their love for the art of ceramics. Every piece tells a story, just like their own.
